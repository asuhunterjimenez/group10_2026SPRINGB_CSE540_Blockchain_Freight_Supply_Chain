Liability for Stolen Vehicles and Customs Penalties

Stolen Vehicles

If any vehicle included in a shipment arranged by the Customer is discovered to be stolen, whether during routine customs inspection or via intelligence-based enforcement action, the Customer shall be held fully responsible for all ensuing penalties, fines, and associated liabilities.

A minimum additional penalty of USD $20,000 per stolen vehicle shall apply, which the Customer acknowledges as a reasonable estimate of G10 Blockchain Freight's damages relating to its working relationship with its shipping partners because of this conduct. This figure is subject to increase depending on the fine or assessment imposed by the customs authority, police, or any investigative agency and reputational risk this might cause to G10 Blockchain Freight .

This is in addition to the USD $10,000 administrative fee charges if your cargo is seized.

In the event that you ship or accept shipments from your customers and move them with G10 Blockchain, and subsequent costs are incurred by G10 Blockchain because of these shipments, you will reimburse G10 Blockchain for all costs, which include but are not limited to legal fees, court costs, fines, freight, demurrage, detention, storage, etc. You further commit to this reimbursement obligation if you are on the booking as the shipper of such shipments or the booking party.

Further, in the event that your shipment is seized by government authorities, you shall pay G10 Blockchain and carriers an additional USD 5000 administrative fee each. You also agree to provide G10 Blockchain the additional documentation that is required for these shipments.

Reputational Standards

The customer further agrees to indemnify G10 Blockchain Freight against all damages (including reputational damages) or loss of carrier privileges that may result from such an incident.

G10 Blockchain Freight will have no obligation to continue processing, clearing, or managing shipments from or on behalf of the Customer under such circumstances .

Governing Terms

All business transacted by G10 Blockchain Services Inc/G10 Blockchain Freight is governed by the Canadian International Freight Forwarders Association's standard trading conditions. A copy is available upon request.
